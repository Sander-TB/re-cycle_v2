About Us

Re-Cycle Tours was founded to show off the beautiful nature of Norway, in a sustainable and healthy way for everyone. Our base is in Oslo, but we plan and host tours all over the country. The tours can vary from a couple of days to a couple of weeks, depending on time of year.


Founded in 2004 by Ola Normann, Re Cycle has done its best to show tourists and locals that it is possible to to have an enjoyable time, whilst also being sustainable and getting exercise.about-content

Partners

Re Cycle is partnered with the Norwegian Tourist Association, to make sure the participants has the best experience as possible, with well planned out routes with loads of beautiful nature to see.


We are also partered with Some Bicycle Brand, making it possible for us to rent out bikes for the tours. It is a trustworthy brand with a lot of good products, so really; if you tour with us, you are gonna have an enjoyable and exiting time that you will never forget about.
